About Our School

Founded in 2013, CHRIST EBBASY Senior High School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community. Our commitment to fostering intellectual growth, character development, and lifelong learning has made us one of the most respected institutions in the region.

With a student body of 1,200 students and a faculty-student ratio of 1:15, we provide personalized attention to ensure each student reaches their full potential.

Our Mission

To empower students to achieve academic excellence, develop strong character, and become responsible global citizens through innovative teaching and a supportive learning environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leader in secondary education that prepares students to meet the challenges of higher education and the modern workforce while contributing positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Integrity and ethical behavior
  • Respect for diversity
  • Community engagement
  • Lifelong learning

Admissions Process

How to Apply

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential, good character, and a commitment to learning. The admissions process includes:

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Submit academic records from previous school
  3. Take our entrance assessment
  4. Participate in an interview (for some programs)
  5. Receive admission decision within 4 weeks

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: January 15
  • Priority Deadline: March 1
  • Final Deadline: April 15
  • Entrance Exams: February-May
